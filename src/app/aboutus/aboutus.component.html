What is Sharelion?

Sharelion in a new, engaging, fun and free way to experience the Australian sharemarket game
without wasting your hard earned money. Sharelion is a real time experience that allows you the
user to get a feeling of what it is like to purchase shares, sell shares, and win big!

Why are we Doing This?

As part of RMIT's Bachelor of IT course, each student must work on and develop one of four
specific project listed and defined by the RMIT IT Project staff. We at Sharelion beleive a
sharemarket simulator game is the most interestiong and provides enough of a challenge for
us to show off our development and program management skills!

How do we Differ?

Here at Sharelion, we aim to bring the best possible experience to our users by providing them
with a simple and easy to use system which requires little to no learning, we also provide an
engaging system for users by giving them an experience which is very similar to real sharemarket
investment experience..... minus spending all that money!

Our Vision!

Our vision is to provide users with a free, easy and fun way to experience what it is like to
invest in shares on the Australian ASX sharemarket. We wish to provide users with a simulation
that simulates the real experience as close as possible!
